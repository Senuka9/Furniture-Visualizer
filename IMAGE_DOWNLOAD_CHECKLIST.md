## Image Quality Tips

### Best Image Characteristics
✓ White or transparent background
✓ Front-facing view (not angled)
✓ Clear and sharp
✓ Professional product photo style
✓ 400x400px minimum (600x600px better)
✓ PNG format (not JPEG)

### Avoid
❌ Images with watermarks
❌ Images with shadows
❌ Very small images (< 300px)
❌ JPEG format (use PNG)
❌ Cluttered backgrounds

## Size Optimization (Optional)

If images are too large (> 1MB each):

### Online Compression
1. Go to tinypng.com
2. Drag & drop your PNG files
3. Download compressed versions
4. Replace in folder

### Keep Original Quality
The current system handles both:
- **Thumbnail (50x50px)** - Auto-scaled from full image

So larger images (600x600px) are actually better!
